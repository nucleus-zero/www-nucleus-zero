About me

I am Mark Curphey. People in the security industry may recognize my name as the founder of OWASP, the Open Web Application Security Project in 2002. Over the last twenty years I have run MSDN (the Microsoft Developer Network), founded an application security company called SourceClear, acquired by Veracode and co-founded a data security company called Open Raven.

In early 2022 I decided to focus on an emerging security area called supply chain security that has already caused havoc on the Internet and set to inflict would for years to come. I am currently helping the Linux Foundation develop tools for and improve awareness of an international open standard (ISO/IEC 5962:2021) for defining software bill of materials.

This site contains articles, links to my public speaking and hosts a newsletter.
